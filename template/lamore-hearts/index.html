our story

First time we met


A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away...

It all started with a cat. A cat named Claudia. It was a cold February afternoon, 5 years ago. I was scrolling aimlessly the news feed while waiting for something to happen during a late shift. I stumbled across a photo with a pretty girl holding a cat. It was Boryana’s cat in the hands of my future love – Eti. A sneaky Like and I moved on. Then I came back and looked at the photo some more. It took me day or two to muster up the courage to wright to her and I finally blabbered something dumb like a cat joke. She responded with way too much laughter. The connection was instant, and we couldn’t stop talking for few days and nights. We bonded over our mutual love for Italian food, good music and our sense of humour. And the rest is history.

Five happy years

It’s been 5 whole years!!! Time just flew past us, as our years together were filled with many challenges. There were ups and downs, laughter and tears and one cute little baby, leading us to the decision that we actually can’t live without each other. We’ve been a family for a year and half now thanks to our wonderful Teddy bear and it’s time to celebrate our love and true commitment.

She said yes!

I had many dreamy ideas on where and when to propose, but I never imagined it will turn out this way. I had the ring for a day with me and it was burning a hole in my pocket. I wanted to propose so badly, but I also wanted some place really romantic. We went on a surprise trip to the Belogradchik Rocks. It was a pretty and warm, autumn day with perfect skies. We were climbing flights of stairs to reach on top of the rocks, carrying my camera backpack and Eti carrying Teddy. Finally, we came to the top and a large vista opened up in front of us. Breathless I took the little red case from my backpack, kneeled down and mumbled something that I really don’t remember. Eti would say I fell on the ground like a sack of potatoes after all those stairs. All I heard was an excited YES! Everything else is a blur.
